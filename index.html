Services

How I can help

Every document tells a story — a family reconnecting, a property being managed, a new chapter unfolding. I ensure that story is told correctly and recognised wherever it needs to go.

01

Powers of Attorney

Preparation, notarisation, and certification of Powers of Attorney for use in Ukraine and other countries. Whether you need to authorise a family member to manage property, handle legal proceedings, or conduct business on your behalf, I will ensure your documents meet all requirements for overseas acceptance.

02

Affidavits

An affidavit is a sworn or affirmed written statement used as evidence in legal proceedings or to satisfy the requirements of an overseas authority. I administer the oath or affirmation, witness your signature, and attach the appropriate notarial certificate — ensuring your affidavit is properly executed and recognised internationally.

03

Company Documents

Businesses operating across borders often need their corporate documents notarised for overseas partners, regulators, or foreign government authorities. I assist with the notarisation and certification of ASIC company extracts, certificates of registration, board resolutions, company constitutions, shareholder agreements, and other corporate records. Where required, I will verify the signatory's authority and the company's status before certifying documents for international use.

04

Certificates of Good Standing of a Corporation

A Certificate of Good Standing confirms that an Australian company is currently registered with ASIC, has met its regulatory obligations, and remains active and compliant. Foreign authorities, banks, business partners, and regulators frequently require this certificate before entering into commercial arrangements with an Australian entity. I prepare and issue notarial certificates of good standing based on verified ASIC records, and can arrange apostille or authentication through DFAT where needed for use overseas.

05

Certifying Documents for Overseas Use

Authentication and certification of documents intended for use outside Australia — including educational qualifications, identity papers, personal certificates such as birth, marriage, and death certificates, immigration records, financial statements, and medical reports. I ensure your documents carry the proper notarial certification for recognition abroad.

06

Apostille & Consular Legalisation

Guidance and assistance with the apostille process through the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT) and consular legalisation where required. I will help you understand which pathway your documents need and support you through each step — from notarisation to final legalisation for use in countries that are party to the Hague Apostille Convention and those that are not.

07

Other Notarial Services

Beyond the services listed above, I am able to assist with a wide range of notarial matters including verification of identity, certifying copies of passports and other identification documents. If you are unsure whether your document requires a Notary Public, please get in touch and I will be happy to advise.
